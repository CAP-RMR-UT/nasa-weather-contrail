Public Affairs
==============


Media Contact
-------------

a. Participants are expected to refer all media inquiries to the mission
   Public Information Officer (PIO).

b. All media communications will be referred to NASA public affairs, mission
   PIO, or the mission IC.


Internal
--------

a. The mission Public Information Officer (PIO) for each incident command
   staff shall be selected by the wing, region, or national Public Affairs
   Officer (PAO).


External
--------

a. News releases will be coordinated with NASA public affairs.
