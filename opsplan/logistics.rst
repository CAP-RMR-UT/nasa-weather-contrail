Logistics
=========


Billeting
---------

a. No billeting expected.


Messing
-------

a. Provided by squadron as required.


Medical
-------

a. Local medical facilities and emergency number 911. Ambulatory patients may
   be transported to medical facilities by CAP vehicle. All other transport
   should be arranged by calling 911.

b. Region or wing medical unit leader shall prepare Medical Plan (ICS Form 206)
   that contains local details to be placed in the briefing packet.


Police and Fire
---------------

a. Local public safety facilities and by emergency number 911.
